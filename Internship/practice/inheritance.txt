inheritance in python:- The class get derived from another class.

simple inheritance:- only one parent and child class.
    a->b

multiple inheritance:- Two parent class and one child class.
    a->b,c

multilevel inheritance:- more than one sub class. 
    a->b->c

hierachical inheritance:- multiple sub classes derive properties from a single super class.
    a->b
    a->c

hybrid inheritance:- combination of mote than one the class.
    a->b->c
       b->d
